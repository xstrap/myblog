Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1962

Church on the Queensway is a Bible-based church that is called to reach the GTA with the life changing message of Jesus Christ. We believe in people having a personal relationship with Jesus and that salvation only comes through Jesus, the Son of God. We believe in the fullness of the Holy Spirit who empowers us to live Godly lives. Our worship services offer dynamic and intimate experiences through music and artistic expressions with relevant biblical teaching that will help you daily live in a relationship with Jesus. We want to see people find new life, hope and healing in Jesus Christ. All of our other programs/ministries are designed to assist you no matter where you are in your spiritual journey. There is something for everyone and every age! We desire to create places where people can encounter God, connect in meaningful relationships, and experience the joy of serving others in our community, our city and our world.

School Auditorium

Was established in 1754

Columbia University, also known as Columbia, and officially as Columbia University in the City of New York, is a private Ivy League research university in New York City. Established in 1754 as King's College on the grounds of Trinity Church in Manhattan, Columbia is the oldest institution of higher education in New York and the fifth-oldest institution of higher learning in the United States. It is one of nine colonial colleges founded prior to the Declaration of Independence, and a member of the Ivy League. Columbia is ranked among the most prestigious universities in the world.
